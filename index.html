About me   Download CV

Hi! I am a PhD candidate in the Technology and Operations department at the University of Michigan - Stephen M. Ross School of Business. My advisors are Samantha Keppler and Stephen Leider. Broadly, I am interested in the way frontline workers learn about and use (or deviate from) new technologies, and the effect of this behavior on operationally important outcomes like accuracy, efficiency, and fairness. Right now, I am using laboratory experiments and qualitative methods to study human-algorithm interactions in service contexts.

Before coming to Ross, I majored in Information Science, Systems, and Technology (ISST) at Cornell Engineering.
