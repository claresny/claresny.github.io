About me

Hi! I am a PhD candidate in the Technology and Operations department at the University of Michigan - Stephen M. Ross School of Business. My advisors are Samantha Keppler and Stephen Leider.

Before coming to Ross, I majored in Information Science, Systems, and Technology at Cornell Engineering. Before that, I grew up in Hanover, NH (no relation), so I love winter sports—especially cross-country skiing!

I am on the 2024-2025 academic job market.
